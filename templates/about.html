About PoultryDetect

PoultryDetect is an AI-powered web application crafted to assist poultry farmers, veterinarians, and students in early disease detection. Our model classifies common diseases such as Coccidiosis, Newcastle Disease, and Salmonella from poultry images, reducing the time taken to respond to outbreaks.

What Makes Us Unique?

  • 🌿 Real-time AI predictions based on deep learning
  • 📸 Intuitive image-based diagnosis for easy use
  • 📚 Educational resources for veterinary training
  • 🌐 Access to curated research literature links
  • 🚜 Built for both academic and rural field use
